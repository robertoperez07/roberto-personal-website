About Me

Based on San Diego, California, I graduated from San Diego State University in August of 2017 with a BS in Computer Science.

My curiosity to discover how the games, websites, and applications that I had been using for years were built eventually led me to pursue a career in software. Exploring such a vast field has given me a new perspective about the work required to develop products and applications that have become essential in today's world, such as cloud computing, electronic commerce, and social media platforms. Modern technologies have opened an enormous world of possibilities for the software industry and I see myself contributing to it in the near future.
